Research Projects

Below are some of my ongoing and completed research projects:

  • Boulder Distributions on Martian Ice Sheets (Sep 2023-present)

    Quantified the distribution of boulders on midlatitude Martian ice sheets, using imagery from the High Resolution Imaging Science experiment (HiRISE).

  • Characterizing Hazards at Candidate Mars Sample Return (MSR) Landing Sites (Jun 2023-Aug 2023)

    Mapped rocks and produced Cumulative Fractional Area (CFA) curves to assess the rockiness of various MSR landing sites (emphasis on the Abercastell (Belva Crater) landing site).

  • Characterizing the Stratigraphy of a Potential Distal Remnant of the Hypanis Delta, Mars (May 2022-Jul 2022)

    Located stratigraphic layers on a potential delta deposit and worked to link stratigraphic relations back to the main delta lobe to determine the extent of the delta.
