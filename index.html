About Me

I'm a first-year graduate research assistant studying boulder distributions on midlatitude Martian ice sheets at the University of Arizona's Lunar and Planetary Lab with Dr. Shane Byrne. My work focuses on analyzing geomorphological data to assess the Martian paleoclimate and water budgets.

Research Interests
